StackScope

StackScope is an independent job market intelligence tool built for developers. It scrapes live postings daily, extracts skill demand data using NLP, and gives you a real picture of where the market is heading so you can make smarter decisions about what to learn and where to apply.

Built by Denzel Chingodza · 2026

By using StackScope you agree to these terms. StackScope is a personal project and is provided free of charge for informational purposes only. All data displayed is collected automatically from publicly accessible job board listings. StackScope does not guarantee the accuracy, completeness, or timeliness of any data shown. Salary figures, skill demand statistics, and trend data are estimates derived from scraped content and should not be used as the sole basis for career or financial decisions. Redistribution or commercial use of any data from this platform without permission is not permitted. These terms may be updated at any time.

Privacy policy

StackScope does not collect, store, or share any personal information from visitors. No account registration is required. No cookies are used for tracking. The skill analyser runs entirely in your browser and no skill selections are transmitted or stored anywhere. External links to job boards are provided for convenience and are governed by those platforms' own privacy policies.
